History and Characters of Shrek

Movie Title Year Released New Main Character(s)
Shrek 2001 Donkey, Princess Fiona, Dragon, Lord Farquaad
Shrek 2 2004 Puss in Boots, Prince Charming, Fairy God Mother, King Harold, Queen Lillian
Shrek the Third 2007 Merlin, Arthur Pendragon, Fergus, Farkle, Felicia
Shrek Forver After 2010 Rumpelstiltskin, Fighter Fiona and other Ogres
While many of the 90's Kids know who Chris Farley is, many of the younger generation does not know the amazing Tommy Boy, Chippendales Try-Out and the one and only Matt Foley, who lives in a Van down by the River. But even 90's kids may not know that Farley was orginally slated to play the beloved Shrek. If it were not for his untimely demise, who knows how Shrek would have grown into our hearts. Even the concept art changed since the Chris Farley rendition of Shrek with Eddie Murphy as Donkey.
